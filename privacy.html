Privacy Policy

Last updated July 14, 2026

SiteSprint is a service of EdgeFinders, LLC (Roswell, Georgia). This policy covers this website and how I handle information when we work together.

This website collects nothing

  • No cookies. None. Not even the "essential" kind.
  • No analytics or trackers. No Google Analytics, no pixels, no fingerprinting.
  • No forms. The only ways to reach me are the email and phone links, which open your own apps.

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, so GitHub's servers may log standard technical data (like IP addresses) the way virtually all web servers do; that's governed by GitHub's privacy statement, and I never see or use it.

If you email or call me

I use what you send me for exactly one thing: answering you and, if you become a client, building your website. Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with anyone, except the bare minimum a service requires (for example, giving a domain registrar your name to register your domain — in your own account).

If you become a client

  • Your business content (text, photos, menus, prices) is used solely to build and maintain your site.
  • Payment goes through PayPal, Venmo, or Zelle — I never see or store card numbers.
  • Want your records deleted after a project? Email me and it's done, minus what tax law requires me to keep (basic invoices).

About my outreach emails

I occasionally email local business owners whose websites are down or missing — individually researched and written, never bought lists. Every one identifies me by name and includes a working opt-out. Say "no thanks" once and you're on my do-not-contact list permanently.
